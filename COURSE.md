# Aru Course

This course is a practical path through **Aru v1.0.0**. It teaches use, not theory first.

Each lesson has one goal, a small pattern, examples, and exercises.

## Lesson 1: Say Something

Goal: make a basic statement.

Pattern:

```text
subject ra predicate
```

Examples:

```text
na ra waro.
I speak.
```

```text
malo ra maru.
The house is big.
```

```text
wai ra nilo.
The water is cold.
```

Exercises:

- Say "I sleep."
- Say "The tool is good."
- Say "The text is simple."

Answers:

```text
na ra miso.
tulu ra nela.
sipu ra lisu.
```

## Lesson 2: Add an Object

Goal: say what an action affects.

Pattern:

```text
subject ra predicate ke object
```

Examples:

```text
na ra sela ke luma.
I see light / the sun.
```

```text
mene ra maku ke malo.
The person builds a house.
```

```text
ta ra lowa ke sipu.
You read a text.
```

Exercises:

- Say "I eat food."
- Say "You hear me."
- Say "The person writes a text."

Answers:

```text
na ra kani ke pani.
ta ra lora ke na.
mene ra raku ke sipu.
```

## Lesson 3: Time

Goal: mark past, present, and future only when needed.

Pattern:

```text
subject ra tense predicate
```

Examples:

```text
na ra pa tanu.
I came.
```

```text
na ra nu kani ke pani.
I am eating food now.
```

```text
na ra po naka.
I will go.
```

Exercises:

- Say "I slept."
- Say "You will come."
- Say "They are reading a text now."

Answers:

```text
na ra pa miso.
ta ra po tanu.
u ra nu lowa ke sipu.
```

## Lesson 4: Negation and Commands

Goal: negate a phrase and give a command.

Rules:

```text
no stands before what it negates
o marks a command
```

Examples:

```text
na ra no kani ke pani.
I do not eat food.
```

```text
na ra pa no tanu.
I did not come.
```

```text
o naka.
Go.
```

```text
o no naka.
Do not go.
```

Exercises:

- Say "Do not eat."
- Say "I do not know."
- Say "You did not speak."

Answers:

```text
o no kani.
na ra no sona.
ta ra pa no waro.
```

## Lesson 5: Noun Groups

Goal: build small descriptive groups.

Pattern:

```text
head modifier
```

Examples:

```text
mene nela
good person
```

```text
malo maru
big house
```

```text
sipu lisu
simple text
```

Exercises:

- Say "new tool."
- Say "cold water."
- Say "quiet person."

Answers:

```text
tulu sini
wai nilo
mene sene
```

## Lesson 6: Relations

Goal: express place, direction, source, means, and weak relation.

Particles:

```text
to -> place / contact
ko -> direction / goal
so -> source
we -> accompaniment / means
ne -> weak relation
```

Examples:

```text
na ra to malo.
I am at the house.
```

```text
na ra naka ko malo.
I go to the house.
```

```text
na ra naka so malo.
I go from the house.
```

```text
na ra salo we tulu.
I work with a tool.
```

```text
malo ne na
my house / house related to me
```

Exercises:

- Say "I go with you."
- Say "The water is from the river."
- Say "My name is Mira."

Answers:

```text
na ra naka we ta.
wai ra so rano.
lani ne na ra ya Mira.
```

## Lesson 7: Context

Goal: put a frame before the main sentence.

Pattern:

```text
frame le, sentence
```

Examples:

```text
temu luma nu le, na ra salo.
Today I work.
```

```text
etu ta ra naka le, na ra naka.
If you go, I go.
```

```text
malo ne na le, ta ra naka ko i.
As for my house, you go to it.
```

Exercises:

- Say "Tomorrow I will come."
- Say "If it is cold, I do not go."
- Say "As for Aru, I study it."

Answers:

```text
temu luma po le, na ra po tanu.
etu u ra nilo le, na ra no naka.
ya Aru le, na ra nema ke i.
```

## Lesson 8: Content Clauses

Goal: say "that..." with one standard construction.

Pattern:

```text
ke i le, sentence
```

Examples:

```text
na ra sona ke i le, u ra pa tanu.
I know that they came.
```

```text
ta ra rima ke i le, na ra naka.
You want me to go.
```

```text
u ra sela ke i le, na ra maku ke malo.
They see that I build a house.
```

Exercises:

- Say "I say that Aru is simple."
- Say "You know that I came."
- Say "They believe that I will come."

Answers:

```text
na ra waro ke i le, ya Aru ra lisu.
ta ra sona ke i le, na ra pa tanu.
u ra sano ke i le, na ra po tanu.
```

## Lesson 9: Relative Phrases

Goal: describe a thing with a small embedded phrase.

Pattern:

```text
head re ... ri ...
```

Examples:

```text
mene re ri ra maku ke malo
person who builds a house
```

```text
malo re na ra sela ke ri
house that I see
```

```text
mene re na ra lano ke pani ko ri
person to whom I give food
```

Exercises:

- Say "tool that I hold."
- Say "person who reads a text."
- Say "house where I am."

Answers:

```text
tulu re na ra kipa ke ri
mene re ri ra lowa ke sipu
malo re na ra to ri
```

## Lesson 10: Questions

Goal: ask yes/no questions and unknown-element questions.

Rules:

```text
ka marks yes/no questions
se marks an unknown element
```

Examples:

```text
ka ta ra kani?
Are you eating?
```

```text
se ra naka?
Who is going?
```

```text
ta ra sela ke se?
What do you see?
```

```text
ta ra no tanu so etu se?
Why did you not come?
```

Exercises:

- Ask "Where are you?"
- Ask "Who reads the text?"
- Ask "Do you know Aru?"

Answers:

```text
ta ra to se?
se ra lowa ke sipu?
ka ta ra sona ke ya Aru?
```

## Lesson 11: Comparison

Goal: compare things without new word forms.

Patterns:

```text
X ra Q pasu Y
X ra Q sama pasu Y
X ra Q mini pasu Y
X ra Q pasu ora
```

Examples:

```text
malo i ra maru pasu malo ena.
This house is bigger than that house.
```

```text
mene i ra riki sama pasu mene ena.
This person is as fast as that one.
```

```text
wai i ra welo mini pasu wai ena.
This water is less hot than that water.
```

Exercises:

- Say "This tool is better than that tool."
- Say "This house is the biggest."
- Say "This text is as simple as that text."

Answers:

```text
tulu i ra nela pasu tulu ena.
malo i ra maru pasu ora.
sipu i ra lisu sama pasu sipu ena.
```

## Lesson 12: Write a Short Text

Goal: combine small sentences into a useful text.

Model:

```text
lani ne na ra ya Mira.
na ra nema ke ya Aru.
na ra rima waro lisu.
temu luma nu le, na ra raku ke sipu mini.
sipu i ra ne ya Aru.
```

Translation:

```text
My name is Mira.
I study Aru.
I want to speak simply.
Today I write a small text.
This text is about Aru.
```

Final exercises:

- Write five sentences about yourself.
- Write five sentences about a house, a tool, and a friend.
- Write a dialogue with at least one question.
- Write one sentence using `re` and one using `ke i le`.
- Keep every sentence short.
