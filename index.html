Learn in 20 Minutes

1. Split the sentence with `ra`

na ra waro.
I speak.

2. Mark the object with `ke`

na ra sela ke luma.
I see light / the sun.

3. Keep relations explicit

na ra naka ko malo.
I go to the house.
