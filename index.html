Minimal speech, clear thought.

Aru is a small constructed language built around regular particles, positional grammar, and short sentences that stay readable.

Learn in 20 Minutes

1. Split the sentence with `ra`

na ra waro.
I speak.

2. Mark the object with `ke`

na ra sela ke luma.
I see light / the sun.

3. Keep relations explicit

na ra naka ko malo.
I go to the house.
