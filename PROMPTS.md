# Writing Prompts

These prompts are designed to grow the Aru corpus without changing the v1.0.0 grammar.

## Prompt 1

Write five sentences about a house near water.

Starter:

```text
na ra raku ke sipu i.
```

## Prompt 2

Describe a person who learns Aru from a friend.

Starter:

```text

```

## Prompt 3

Starter:

